Most viewers at this exhibition will immediately recognize this place. The Tower Theater is a local celebrity of a building. I took this photo while my wife and I were just visiting Sacramento, before we took the plunge and moved here.

It was lucky timing that the theater was showing Ladybird, a movie directed by another Sacramento celebrity. This makes this my "most Sacramento" photo ever.

We ended up seeing Thoroughbreds because we had already seen Ladybird.
