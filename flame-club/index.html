About:

The Flame Club is on the long list of places in Sacramento that I've photographed but never actually been inside of. Actually that is not quite true, I did pop my head in the door after taking this photo. Pretty sure a needle scratched, the music stopped, and everyone turned to look at me as I slunk back out the door.

In all seriousness I would like to check it out someday, there are just so many great spots to grab a drink in Sac.
