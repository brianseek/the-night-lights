About:

San Francisco is a great city to wonder around looking for night lights. It feels like there are so many cocktail lounges and dive bars that each one needs to one-up the next with their illuminated beacons. They are out to draw in thirsty revelers but it also draws us folks with hungry eyes and our cameras.

This is one of several photos that features San Francisco in this exhibit. Can you spot the others?
