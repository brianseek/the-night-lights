About:

This photograph is the one that made me decide to dedicate a lot of time and effort to this body of work. It was the first one that really wowed me when I say it floating there in the tray of developer.

There have been many iterations of this print and I am really happy with this version. Its been a lot of work in the darkroom to get the bricks on the side of the building just right with the balance of keeping the highlights in the sign read-able. The other challenge is getting the dark sky and clouds to look right. I'm sure there will be another evolution of this print, but for now I am proud of what is shown here.

Feel free to drop me an email if you want to know anything else.
