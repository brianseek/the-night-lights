Another jaunt down Main Street in Mesa from when I first started working on these night lights. The name of the motel is obscured by the overblown highlights. This was before I got my technique dialed in for metering these high contrast scenes and printing highlights. While not perfect, I still really like the print. It's a good reminder of my journey with these photos.

The name of the motel is "Trava-Leers".
