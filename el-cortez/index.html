About:

New Mexico is a great little weekend trip if you are living in Arizona. We took a spur of the moment trip to soak in the hot springs along the Rio Grande. Truth or Consequences is very similar to the little town that I grew up in along the border of AZ and NM. I found several night lights on this trip. I selected another one to be in this exhibit as well.

The three pillars of my night lights are bars, motels, and theaters. This is a really great one, the motif fits in perfect with the small southwestern town. You just can't go wrong taking a night photo of a beautiful old neon sign.

I did not see Justice League.
