About:

See the page for Fidel to get a bit of the back story on my trip to Cuba.

I walked by this pollo stand a ton of times before deciding to take a photograph. It seemed like there was always a car in front or a bunch of people milling around.

I really enjoy the details of the wall behind the stand in the shadows. The interesting characters that you can start to make out as you look closer add to mood of this one.

Unfortunately I did not sample the pollo.
