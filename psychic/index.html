About:

This photograph was from one of my first trips to Sacramento while still living in Arizona. I took several night lights photos in this area, maybe that helped inspire the move to Sacramento.

This is one of those scenes that wouldn't work so well in the day time. There is something about lit neon signs that almost convinces me that the person inside could offer supernatural insight. Almost.
