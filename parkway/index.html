About:

The Parkway Motel is a sight I stumbled upon one night during a road trip. My wife and I took a trip to see the sights of Bryce Canyon and Zion. Those parks are beautiful, but the roadside along the way also offers many gems.

This is a photo that wasn't printed until several years after it was taken. It wasn't until I had set up my darkroom here in Sacramento did I think about printing it. I can probably thank the pandemic for making me dig back through some old negatives for something to print.

Feel free to drop me an email if you want to know anything else.
