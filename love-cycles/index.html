About:

Love Cycles was a really great custom motorcycle shop in Phoenix. The owner is a real nice guy and let me and a friend photograph all around the shop one night. As we were leaving, I setup the tripod and shot this photo.

This shop on Grand Avenue has now closed up, but Jeremiah is still building bikes under the Love Cycles banner. I gave him one of these prints as a thank you for letting us bumble around his shop for a while.

Feel free to drop me an email if you want to know anything else.
