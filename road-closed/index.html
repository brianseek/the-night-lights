Road Closed

Location:
Knoxville, Tennessee
Year:
2017
About:

I got this job at an ad agency that was based in Knoxville, so they flew me out for an orientation. I had already sworn off working at ad agencies, but I needed a job and thought I should give it another chance.

Anyway, I'm in Knoxville for a few days and have the evenings and nights pretty much to myself to explore downtown Knoxville looking for night lights. On the very first night, I slipped on some icy concrete stairs. I saved my camera and equipment but completely destroyed my phone that was in my back pocket. It literally bent at a near 45 degree angle. I usually use my phone to time these shots, so I was left to counting in my head for these 10 second to 2 minute exposures.

It ended up working out fine, but I was then stuck for a few days without a phone in a city where I didn't know anyone other than the people I just met at the job.

I ended up quitting a few months later. I was right about ad agencies and haven't worked at one since. Anyway this photo was almost edited out but I liked the way it hangs next to Impala Alley so here it is.

Feel free to drop me an email if you want to know anything else.
