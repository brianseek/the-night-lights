See the page for Fidel to get a bit of the back story on my trip to Cuba.

I walked by this 1959 Impala a few times during the day and kept logging a mental note to come back at night. I've always really liked the 4 door hardtop/flattop GM body style. Its not a body style you see often.

I'm very pleased with the way this photograph came out, its one of my personal favorites in this series. 1950s American cars are such a cliche for Cuba photos, but it's a cliche for a reason!
